### Research

I work at the intersection of machine learning and clinical neuroscience,
building predictive models and analysis tools for epilepsy surgery evaluation.
My focus is on reproducibility, robustness across centres, and accessibility.

**Interests:** functional connectivity · network biomarkers · EEG · clinical ML · reproducible science
